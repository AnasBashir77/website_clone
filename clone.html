About


Let me introduce myself, my name is Sylvie and my dogs Opale and Soda.
I have loved animals since I was little. I decided to give my time in a shelter to walk our dear dogs and give them a little of my affection.
I thus adopted my little soda with his behavioral problems. After a long life of monotonous work, I decided to take the plunge and move towards the profession that made sense to me, and it is the work of dog trainer that made me reborn.

Why its prices?

As a dog trainer, my rates are based on several factors: Extensive experience in the field of dog training and I have completed a 10-month training course to obtain certification.
My services go beyond simple dog training sessions, such as personalized assessments, detailed advice, regular follow-ups... Costs associated with my business management, such as travel expenses, professional insurance, charges (on a balance sheet of 70 euros, URSSAF takes 15) When I lead a one-hour session, I invest myself fully,
giving everything I have, up to 100%. I particularly appreciate these moments of exchange where the people who use my services trust me. It is my duty to satisfy them, even if sometimes it can be difficult, because I remind you, I work with living beings.
What I also like is meeting new people. Even though we work with the dog, we can also discuss other topics. And it is very enriching, because people bring me a lot too, through their life stories. Ultimately, this message was simply to emphasize that I understand that this may seem expensive to some people. I understand that sometimes, people are surprised by the rates charged, but it is important to take into account all the aspects (time, investment, costs ...) associated with my work.
Thank you.

On vacation

Even though summer is coming and we want to have fun and rest, we must not forget our little ones. A little check-up can sometimes be necessary to
solve small problems. For more serious concerns, sessions may be necessary, but
even with the heat, we do not give up. We must take the time and be patient, even if it can be difficult sometimes. I remain available for any additional information. Thank you.
